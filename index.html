Precarious Platforms

by: Branson Boock

This game was made for my level 2 project at The League of Amazing Programmers.
Click here to download and play!

1. Why I Made This Game

I thought of a game that people would try to beat their friends scores and would have a lot of fun playing. I took inspiration from Mario and other platform games. I ended up making a mix of mostly platform and runner, but also a little bit of rougelike.

2. What I Learned

I learned that people want easy to understand colors and easy to navigate menus as well as Easter eggs to keep people playing. All of these led to changes with the menus and display.

3. Architecture of My Game

The platforms make the ground and hard obstacles and pair with the dispenser which are mostly the same besides the part that they shoot deadly arrows. The baddies and death-blocks are like spikes and a moving spike-ball, they work to make a challenging run. The arrows also add a bit more spice to the jumping and running from the deadly screen-scroll rock.

4. Technical Implementation

The generation system is one of the best and worst features. It implements all the features in working condition, but with my know this also makes it unbalanced because anything can spawn and move almost anywhere and make it impossible. It uses a large system of for loops and randoms to create a slightly moderated generation. First, we generate a number of objects to generate. Next, we generate the x and y. Only some objects have a random width and height. Next for baddies we generate their movement distance and if they are moving left and right or up and down. AT this point we also generate if the arrow dispenser point left or right. Finally, we repeat from step 2 until we reach the number from step 1.We do this for most types of objects in the game.

5. Most Challenging Part

The most challenging part of this project was getting player mechanics to work. We had to make the player have collision, walk, jump, crouch, center the display around the player, and balance it all. We faced difficult changes with the camera not moving and having to move it all, changing the display of all the objects,and get the player to jump and have mock physics. We took it piece by piece, making changes to how we did things to optimize and get it to work with newer features.

6. Future Fixes and Updates

I would redo the art and balance the generation system so the game is possible to go past 10 points. I would also redo the menus to make the look of the game nicer. That is really all I would change.
